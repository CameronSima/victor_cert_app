The Contemporary Priestess Statement of Application

American Priestess' ordained priestesses are sanctioned by the state of California to hold the professional title as religious clergy, and thus may assume all associated government rights and benefits, and legally perform many services such as:

_ Officiate marriages

_ Perform intuitive/psychic readings and spiritual counseling

_ Perform intuitive/psychic/spiritual healings

This license is valid for performing weddings throughout the United States.

Certified and licensed priestesses perform many other services such as:

_ Officiate unions (commitment ceremonies, hand-fastings, etc.)

_ Officiate divorce ceremonies

_ Provide Spiritual Midwifery services during a soul’s transition through times of birth, blood rites, loss, hospice and more

_ Perform Personal and Public Ritual Ceremonies: Goddess Worship Services, Rituals of Earth Reverence, Weddings, Handfastings, Unions, Birth, Menses, Divorce, Funerals, Seasonal Celebrations, and more

_ Produce programs intended to be spiritually uplifting for the community —adults, teens and children
